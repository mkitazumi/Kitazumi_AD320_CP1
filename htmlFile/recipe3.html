Butter Mochi

By Someone

  • 1 box mochiko flour (16 oz)
  • 2 Â½ cups sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1 block butter (melted)
  • 5 eggs (beat prior to adding)
  • 1 tsp. vanilla
  • 1 can coconut milk (12 oz but 14 oz ok)
  • 1 cup shredded coconut (optional)

Mix/whisk dry ingredients. Mix other ingredients together. ( i just put all ingredients in a bowl and use a hand mixer)

Pour into a well greased 9x13 pan. Tap pan to remove air bubbles. Batter rises so place the pan in the middle of the oven. it will deflate. (sometimes overflows in a pyrex, a pan with taller edges is better)

Bake at 350F for about 1 hour (test with toothpick, should be nice and brown)

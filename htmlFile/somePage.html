Chicken Long Rice

by Hawaiian Electric (i think? its written by hand and mom forgot)

  • 32oz of chicken brth and 4 chicken breasts

    OR whole chicken leftovers

  • 3 tbsp shouyu
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 3 tbsp grated ginger
  • 10 oz vermicelli noodles
  • 1/4 green onion
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • some green onions for garnish

    • If you are using leftover chicken with bones: boil chicken with some ginger and garlic. Boil till meat is falling off bones. Drain broth into another pot and pick off meat.

    Soak vermicelli in a container with water. Make sure the water is above the noodles. The noodles will soak up a lot of water.

    Over mediumheat in chicken broth add shoyu, sugar, garlic ginger. If you are using chicken thighs then add them in now. Add in salt and pepper to taste.

    Fish out chicken when the meat is done cooking. Turn stove to low. Shred meat and discard bones.

    When the chicken is shredded, add back into pot with vermicelli noodles and cook for about 5 minutes or when noodles are done.

    When serving, garnish with chopped green onions.
